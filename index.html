About Delivery

Yes, we deliver orders directly to customers on scheduled days. We also use bus stations for extended areas like Prampram, Dawhenya, Ashaiman, and Afienya, as well as other far areas within Ghana.
Delivery is free for orders above GHC 1,000. Orders below this amount attract a fee based on location. All orders going to bus stations or parcel offices attract an extra GHC 15 fee.
Customers in far areas within Ghana can receive their orders through bus stations or parcel offices. Deliveries to these stations (e.g., Circle, Kaneshie) attract an additional GHC 15 fee.
Deliveries are made between 10:00am and 5:00pm on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays.
